GIRI PRADHAKSHANAM

Giri Pradakshina, or Girivalam, is a sacred circumambulation of Arunachala Hill in Tiruvannamalai, revered as a manifestation of Lord Shiva. Spanning 14 kilometers, devotees walk barefoot, typically during full moon nights, in a profound act of devotion and surrender. The path features eight sacred lingams, known as Ashta Lingams, each symbolizing directions and cosmic energies. This journey is seen as a path to spiritual awakening, helping devotees shed ego and connect with the divine.

SRI SESHADRI SWAMY ASHRAM

The Sri Seshadri Swamy Ashram, located in Tiruvannamalai near the sacred Arunachala Hill, is dedicated to Sri Seshadri Swamy, a revered saint known as the "Saint with the Golden Hand" for his miraculous blessings. The ashram serves as a tranquil retreat for devotees seeking spiritual solace and guidance. It houses the samadhi of Sri Seshadri Swamy, where devotees offer prayers and meditate in peaceful surroundings.

ADHI ANNAMALAI

Adhi Annamalai Temple, located in Tamil Nadu, is an ancient and sacred Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva. It is believed to be older than the renowned Arunachaleswarar Temple in Thiruvannamalai. According to legends, this temple was built even before Lord Shiva manifested as the Arunachala Hill. The temple is revered for its spiritual significance, attracting devotees seeking peace and enlightenment.
